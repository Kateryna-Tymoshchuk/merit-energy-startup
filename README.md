## Overview
Merit Energy is a startup concept focused on helping households optimise energy usage and EV charging through AI-driven insights.

The project was developed as part of an MSc in Digital Business and demonstrates end-to-end startup development: from problem discovery and product vision to prototype design and go-to-market strategy.

> This repository showcases the *process, thinking, and execution* behind the project.  
> Sensitive commercial details and full financial models are intentionally excluded.

## Problem
Households with electric vehicles face rising energy costs, complex tariffs, and inefficient charging behaviour.

## Solution
An AI-powered mobile platform that:
- Analyses household energy patterns  
- Optimises EV charging schedules  
- Provides personalised cost-saving recommendations  
- Supports sustainable energy behaviour
